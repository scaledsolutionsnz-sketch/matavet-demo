About MVS EquiBreed

Expert reproduction care
for mares and stallions.

MVS EquiBreed is directed by Dr Ria Harteveld MSc, with consultant support from Dr Lee Morris BVSc DVSc DipACT — a Registered Specialist in Veterinary Reproduction.

Our purpose-built facility is located adjacent to the MVS Equine Hospital at 19 Puketutu Road, Matamata — providing seamless access to the full range of diagnostic and medical support our hospital offers.

We serve mares, stallions, and youngstock. Our MPI-approved quarantine facility ensures stallion collections meet the highest standards of care and compliance for both domestic and export semen.

Reproduction Services

Comprehensive care across
all aspects of breeding.

💉
Artificial Insemination

Expert AI management for optimal conception rates. We handle fresh, chilled, and frozen semen inseminations with careful cycle monitoring and timing to maximise your mare's chances of conception.

🧬
Embryo Transfer

Advanced fresh and frozen embryo solutions for mares that cannot carry a foal to term or where multiple foals per season from a single mare are desired. Our team has extensive experience with both fresh and frozen embryo programmes.

🔍
Fertility Diagnosis & Treatment

Expert assessment and tailored solutions to overcome breeding challenges. We investigate and treat conditions affecting mare and stallion fertility, offering evidence-based management plans to improve outcomes.

🐎
Stallion Services

Comprehensive semen collection, evaluation, freezing, and distribution via our MPI-approved facility. Stallion training and on-site collections in a purpose-built, compliant quarantine environment ensuring the highest standards for domestic and export semen.
