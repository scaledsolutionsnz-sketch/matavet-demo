About MVS Equine

A dedicated team built around your horse's wellbeing.

Matamata Veterinary Services Equine has built a reputation across Waikato and beyond for delivering exceptional care — combining clinical expertise with genuine passion for horses and the people who care for them.

Our team includes veterinarians with specialist expertise in racing, performance, surgery, foal care, and reproduction, supported by veterinary interns, nurses, technicians, and administrative staff dedicated to your horse's care.

Our Facilities

Purpose-built for equine excellence.

Our hospital at 362 Hinuera Road features everything needed for advanced equine care — from state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment to surgical theatres, post-op recovery, and extensive stable and paddock facilities.

✦
Advanced Diagnostic Imaging Digital radiography, ultrasonography, and nuclear scintigraphy (bone scan)
✦
Surgical Theatres Standing procedures and full general anaesthetic surgery capabilities
✦
Extensive Grounds Post-and-rail yards, paddocks, and stabling for in-patient care
✦
MVS EquiBreed — 19 Puketutu Road MPI-approved reproduction facility adjacent to the equine hospital
