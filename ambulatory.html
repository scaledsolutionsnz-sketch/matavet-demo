Care at Your Property

Bringing the clinic to your horse.

Our ambulatory veterinarians travel throughout the Waikato and surrounding regions, bringing portable diagnostic equipment so your horse can be examined and treated in the environment it knows best.

Whether it's a routine health visit, a lameness investigation, a dental check, or a pre-purchase examination — our experienced vets provide the same high standard of care in the field as in our hospital.

What We Offer

Ambulatory services
in detail.

🦴
Digital Radiography

Computer-based X-ray imaging using portable field equipment. Used for investigating lameness, fractures, joint disease, and pre-purchase assessments. Results available immediately on site.

🔊
Ultrasonography

High-frequency sound wave imaging for soft tissue evaluation, tendon and ligament assessment, lameness investigation, and reproductive examinations — all performed with the horse standing.

🦷
Equine Dentistry

Full dental care from routine annual rasping and examination through to the diagnosis and treatment of complex dental disorders, including extractions and surgical procedures when required.

📋
Pre-Purchase Examinations

Independent PPE assessments prior to purchase. We offer the two-stage examination for pleasure and low-level competition horses, and the five-stage examination for performance horses and racehorses. Supplementary testing including radiography, ultrasonography, endoscopy, and blood screening is available.

🩺
Preventative Health Care

Vaccination programmes, parasite management, nutritional and management advice, and general wellness examinations to keep your horses performing at their best throughout the year.

🏇
Lameness Investigation

Thorough lameness evaluations including gait analysis, flexion tests, nerve blocks, and advanced imaging. From subtle performance issues to acute lameness — we find the cause.

🚑
Acute Illness & Emergencies

Prompt ambulatory response for colic, wounds, fevers, and other acute presentations. Our team is available 24/7 for emergency field calls across the Waikato.

⚕️
Minor Surgical Procedures

Standing surgical procedures that can be safely and effectively performed in the field environment, avoiding the need for referral to hospital in many cases.
